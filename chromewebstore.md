The Librarian for your Browser Tabs.
IntelliTab is a privacy-first tab manager that uses the world's most advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to intelligently categorize, group, and declutter your browser in real-time. Whether you’re a developer with 50 documentation tabs or a student juggling multiple research projects, IntelliTab restores order with a single click.

Bring Your Own AI
IntelliTab is provider-agnostic. Connect your own API keys for Gemini, Claude, OpenAI, Groq, or OpenRouter. We store your keys locally, and nothing leaves your machine except the API calls you explicitly initiate.

A Personal "SOUL" for your Workspace
Standard tab managers are passive. IntelliTab is an active librarian. By defining a "SOUL" (a set of core behavioral truths), you teach the AI exactly how your digital workspace should look. It learns from your habits, respects your custom rules, and maintains your standards automatically.

Getting Started:
Install the extension.
Select your AI Provider (Gemini, Claude, OpenAI, or Groq) in the Options page and enter your API Key.
Click "Analyze Tabs" and watch the chaos disappear.
Use the Feedback Chat to retrain the librarian if it misses a spot.
Clean your digital desk. Boost your focus. Try IntelliTab today.
