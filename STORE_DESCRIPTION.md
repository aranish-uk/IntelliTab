# IntelliTab: AI-Powered Browser Organization

**Stop losing focus in a sea of tabs. Let AI do the cleaning for you.**

IntelliTab is a next-generation tab manager that uses advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) to intelligently categorize, group, and declutter your browser window in real-time. Whether you're a developer with 50 documentation tabs or a student juggling multiple research projects, IntelliTab restores order with a single click.
